Welcome to Hell Bent Gaming

Horror has been a popular genre in literature for hundreds of years, and in recent decades in films as well. By comparison video games have not received as much attention by the mainstream public.

The appeal of the spectrally macabre is generally narrow because it demands from the reader a certain degree of imagination and a capacity for detachment from everyday life. Relatively few are free enough from the spell of the daily routine to respond to tappings from outside, and tales of ordinary feelings and events, or of common sentimental distortions of such feelings and events, will always take first place in the taste of the majority - Howard Philips Lovecraft (1927)

Hell Bent was created to recognise the niche genre of the growing (but still marginally disregarded) gaming industry and to inform visitors and help them gain a wider appreciation for horror in video games and the horror genre in general. Particularly this site is to celebrate the storytelling, art and diversity of what may otherwise appear to be an obscure sub-genre.

Included in this site

Horror in video games continues to grow and so this website aims to expand to keep users up to date with the latest horror titles. Users can sign up for newsletters via our Form page to receive weekly content updates and the latest news in the video game industry.

Refer to the Content page for story synopsis of some of the horror video game genre's major franchises, as well as a full list of all of the horror games currently available to-date.
