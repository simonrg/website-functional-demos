Introduction

My topic is the World Wide Web Consortium. For the site content I focused on giving a brief of what the W3C represents; an open forum for the continued development of the Web. This covers a brief history of the major developments that lead to the founding of the W3C. The second part then includes the latest picture of the team behind administering the W3C. It also includes the three hosts of the W3C, and a list of countries where the W3C has offices. The final section is about the certification process of the W3C. Because the W3C is a Worldwide organisation, the details about the certification process are an important part in exemplifying the transparent nature of the organisation and underline the principles the W3C is based upon; an organisation that involves all people who want to help shape the development of the Web.
